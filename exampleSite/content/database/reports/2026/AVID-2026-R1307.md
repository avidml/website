## Description

Stack overflow in paddle.searchsorted (CVE-2023-52304)

## Details

Stack overflow in paddle.searchsorted in PaddlePaddle before 2.6.0. This flaw can lead to a denial of service, or even more damage.

Reason for inclusion in AVID: CVE-2023-52304 concerns PaddlePaddle, a major AI framework. The vulnerability is a software defect (stack overflow) in a component used within ML pipelines, with potential denial of service and broader impact. This sits squarely in the software supply chain for general-purpose AI systems, and there is public CVE/NVD advisory coverage. Therefore, it meets criteria for AI relevance, supply-chain relevance, security risk, and sufficient evidence.
