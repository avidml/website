## Description

Stack overflow in paddle.linalg.lu_unpack (CVE-2023-52307)

## Details

Stack overflow in paddle.linalg.lu_unpack in PaddlePaddle before 2.6.0. This flaw can lead to a denial of service, or even more damage.

Reason for inclusion in AVID: CVE-2023-52307 describes a stack overflow in PaddlePaddle's lu_unpack, a vulnerability in a widely-used ML framework. This directly concerns AI/ML software stacks and their dependencies, impacting systems that build/train/package/deploy AI models. It is a security vulnerability with potential DoS and other impacts, and the issue resides in a software component (PaddlePaddle) used in general-purpose AI pipelines, satisfying the AI-related, supply-chain, and vulnerability criteria. The cited CVE/NVD details provide sufficient signal for classification.
