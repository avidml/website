## Description

TheLibrarian.io Internal Cloud Environment Access via web_fetch Tool

## Details

The web_fetch tool is intended to accept a public URL and retrieve the contents. When asked to retrieve the contents from a private destination (such as http://localhost), the AI rightly refuses. Unfortunately, it can be easily tricked and subverted. ‍By providing encoded or non-obvious variations, the AI does not validate the input and proceeds to point its fetching client inward, thereby exposing internal information.
